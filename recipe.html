Tasty Momo Recipe

Ingredients

Here are the essential Ingredients for making delicious momo:

  • Dough Cups All purpose flour
  • water (as needed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Fillings:500gm chicken or cabbage
  • 1 small onion ,finely chopped
  • 2 GARLIC Cloves,Minced
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger paste
  • 1 Green chilly, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt and pepper and Timmur to taste
  • 1 tablespoon soya sauce

Directions

  1. prepare the dough; In a bowl, mix the flour with salt. Gradually and water and knead until you form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the fillings; In another bolw, combine the chicker or cabbage and chopped onion, garlic, ginger and green chilly. Season with salt pepper and soya according to your taste.
  3. divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circular disc.
  4. Place a spoonful of fillings in the center of each disc
  5. Fold the edges and press to seal properly
  6. Steam the momo in steamer for around 15-30 minutes
